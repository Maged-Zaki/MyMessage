Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does MyMessage ensure the security of my messages?

A: MyMessage employs end-to-end encryption, which means that your messages are securely encrypted on your device and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient. This ensures that no one, including MyMessage, can access the content of your messages.

Q: How can I verify if my messages are encrypted?

A: MyMessage uses end-to-end encryption by default for all messages. You can verify this by checking for the presence of encryption indicators, such as a lock icon or a message indicating that the conversation is secured, within the app.

Q: Are voice and video calls also encrypted?

A: Yes, voice and video calls made through MyMessage are also encrypted using end-to-end encryption. This ensures that your calls are private and cannot be intercepted or accessed by unauthorized parties.

Q: Can MyMessage access my messages or share them with third parties?

A: No, MyMessage cannot access your messages or share them with third parties. The end-to-end encryption ensures that only you and the intended recipient can read the messages. MyMessage does not have access to the encryption keys required to decrypt the messages.

Q: What should I do if I suspect a security breach or encounter any issues?

A: If you suspect a security breach or encounter any issues, please contact our support team immediately. You can reach us via email at support@MyMessage.com or by calling our dedicated helpline at +1 123-456-7890. Our team will assist you in resolving any security concerns or technical difficulties.
